Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information such as your user ID, username, and any data included in the log files you upload. This data is only used to display Discord logs in a readable format on the site.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the purpose of converting and displaying the uploaded logs. We do not share or sell your information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We take precautions to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We do not store your uploaded logs beyond the session in which they are processed. Once you leave the site, your data is erased from our system.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. If any changes are made, they will be posted on this page.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us via Discord or email.
